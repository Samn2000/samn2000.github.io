An Overview of Callums Corner

Read Time:15 Minutes

Callum Adams, formerly known as Sanjay Bennett, (born September 26, 1981), better known online as Callum's Corner, is an English YouTube vlogger who rose to fame, particularly in British internet culture, after he published a rant video threatening people who joked that his head was shaped like a Tic Tac.

'Ayup and welcome to Callum's corner!' Callum's Intro

His most popular video is titled, "MY HEAD DOES NOT LOOK LIKE A SODDING TIC  TAC. A FINAL WARNING" and was uploaded on July 20, 2016. On October 22, 2016 he uploaded a video titled "My head does look like a sodding tic tac. You win, I give up." where he gave into the viewers who believed his head looked like "a sodding tic tac", and admitted it did look like one. Callum created a second channel called "Callum Does The Internet" on November 16, 2016, which is used less often. Videos on that channel include live subreddit reviews or investigations into problematic content creators, such as Paul Breach and Ian Moore. It is also now used for his Meme Review series, having previously uploaded episodes on his main channel.

He initially uploaded videos on Facebook and YouTube by making short videos sharing advice and life stories. However, it wasn't until WillNE reacted to his infamous "Tic Tac" video that he attracted the large audience that he upholds to this day. This original video, the following diss track created on Callum by BGMedia artist Afghan Dan (with the help of WillNE), and his innumerable appearances in other YouTubers' videos have cemented his place as an icon in the UK YouTube scene.

Personal Life

Callum Adams was born on 26 September 1981 in Blackburn in the North of England to a working class family. His family moved to the Isle of Wight when he was six months old, where he spent a large amount of his childhood. When he was young, his father was exposed as having a secret second family with another woman, leading to his parents' divorce. His mother forced him to change his name to a "spirit" name, including taking a name from a different race, and her maiden name (Bennett) - he chose the name "Sanjay" because he 'liked curries'. Callum later said he regretted this decision as it led to bullying when popular Eastenders character Sanjay Kapoor was revealed to be impotent. After school, he moved to Surrey where he went to Reigate College, as stated on one of his live streams.

Not a lot is known about Callum's life through his twenties to now. He has previously said that he used to be a insurance broker, and that he was engaged for a period of time. At some point after college he moved to Bristol, where his mother lived with him as he took care of her. He also worked as a carer in at least 2 care homes during this time; he quit his last care job in Bristol over his hatred for it. People eventually found out Callum's address in Bristol; this resulted in sex workers, Tesco Clubcard's and toasters being sent to his house en masse, the latter of which came after his audience asked to see his kitchen appliances on live stream.

He moved back to the Isle of Wight in July 2020 to a small village called Bembridge. In August 2021, trolls started review bombing and changing the names of locations in his village which caused a shop owner in the village to confront him about the reviews. In June 2022, a troll anonymously emailed his local churchwarden claiming Callum was a sexual deviant that preyed on elderly men, lived out sexual fantasies as a merman, abused marine animals and abducted strangers while roleplaying as Michael Jackson. In October 2023, he moved house again to an undisclosed part of the Isle of Wight; he also started working as a carer again. In June 2024, he was once again found by trolls when someone posted an allegation of paedophilia to the family of a person he cared for - as a result of this, Callum is now considering quitting his job and emigrating to Thailand.

Callum has two sisters, one unnamed, and rarely described as anything other than 'the one that [he] likes' and his other sister 'Aimee' who is often the antagonist of his videos. Aimee is described by Callum as 'life ruining' and her 'militant veganism' fuels Callum's thematic opposition to veganism. Callum has two nieces (children of Aimee) and one nephew (son of the other sister, that he does like), Eddie, who has appeared in a large amount of Callum's videos before stopping due to sexually inappropriate memes about him and Callum. Callum also has multiple cousins called including Gregory and Tim, though Callum's relation to Gregory has been contended.

Callum also regularly streams on his Twitch account, where he plays games such as 'Wii Sports Resort' and 'Crash Bandicoot', even when the community does not like the game he is playing. He also does variety streams such as Sleep Streams, VR Streams and rare Hot Tub streams. He constantly gets 'troll donations' where viewers spend the minimum amount of money ($3) to send a text to speech message or a video link which will play on stream. This feature is meant for songs and music but often abused to play racist songs, AI-generated music, and other types of filth often regarding prominent figures in the community such as Elliot Rodger, Ian Moore, Jimmy Savile, and up until recently, Minitom. More recently, Callum has also been exposed to gore through his Twitch donations. In June 2022, Callum was banned from using Streamlabs due to extremely inappropriate donations, including one where a penis was shown on stream. He now uses StreamElements.
